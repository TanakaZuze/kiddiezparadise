Extracurricular activities

We offer a range of extracurricular activities that you can add at an extra cost

Gym Champs

Gym Champs offers a range of special motor skills classes with a clear emphasis on the joy on the joy of movement.The pre-school program is designed to encourage whole body exercise including development in strength,flexibility and coordination.Classes are structured,but not channel their physical energy while learning to socialize with other students in a safe and fun environment

Soccer Champs

Soccer Champs is a program designed to assist every child in improving their skills and movement.The program provides necessary fundamental skills which later on allow a child to venture into other types of sports easily if they wish.Lessons are well structured allowing learners to reach their perfect potential of executing skills and implementing ideas while having fun.Most importantly the program encourages teamwork and sportsmanship
